About Me

Exploring the intersection of intelligence, systems, and narrative.

Hello! I'm Brendon Hawkins. My work revolves around understanding complex systems – whether they're geopolitical threats, social dynamics, or the narratives that shape our world. I started in strategic intelligence and training, focusing on how to analyze information and anticipate future developments.

Over time, my interests broadened. I realized that the tools of intelligence analysis could be applied more widely, helping individuals and organizations navigate complexity in various domains. This led me to explore systems thinking, design principles, and the power of storytelling as ways to make sense of and influence the systems we inhabit.

My Philosophy

I believe in the power of clarity and structured thinking. In a world saturated with information, the ability to discern patterns, understand underlying structures, and communicate insights effectively is crucial. My goal is to develop and share frameworks, tools, and narratives that enhance this capability.

Whether through consulting, training (via IndependINT), developing civic tools, or writing fiction, the common thread is a desire to equip people with better ways to think about and engage with the world.

This Site

This website serves as a central point for these diverse activities. It's a place to share insights (Blog), showcase projects, and connect with others interested in similar ideas. Explore the different sections to see how these pieces fit together.
