The Augmented

I wrote my first novel, The Augmented, in 2018. It is from an idea I had in 2013 which was inspired by the article How Companies Learn Your Secrets. I spend about a year trying to get it traditionally published with no success. In 2021 my editor insisted that I self publish, so I put on my project managment hat and got to work.

The self publishing process was a lot of fun. I found a fantastic cover designer who gave me exactly what I asked for, before then suggesting this other cover which was even better.

I'm happy to share it with you, feel free to download it and read it.

Genre: Science Fiction
